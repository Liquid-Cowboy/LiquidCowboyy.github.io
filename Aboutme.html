About Me

Hi! My name’s Marcos Teixeira AKA Liquid Cowboy, i’m 24 y.o. and I’m from Porto, Portugal.

I’ve always been interested in the art world, with special focus in music and visual arts.

Since highschool I’ve been pretty serious about drawing and painting, always looking forward to having a career related to illustration and sequencial art/ comic books. I am also a cinema and photography afficionado, having finished my highschool with a specialization on that subject. In what languages are concerned, my English (learned mostly through movies and videogames) is pretty decent and I also speak a bit of French.

Welcome to my website, I hope you find joy in what I do.
